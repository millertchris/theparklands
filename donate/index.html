Let’s keep a good thing growing.

Annual operations of The Parklands are estimated to cost around $4.5 million per year, which is supported by donations from individuals, businesses and foundations, in addition to facility rentals and program fees. Anyone who donates to The Parklands automatically becomes one of our Parklands Members. To join or renew your Membership, simply make a donation in your name, anonymously, as a gift, or in honor of someone else. All donations are 100% tax-deductible.

The Parklands Fund

Sustain sustainability.

When you become a Member of The Parklands, you are supporting The Parklands Fund, an annual campaign that allows us to stay open 365 days a year, free of charge. The Parklands Fund helps care for the 77,980 trees planted through 2019, the management of nearly 2,000 acres of forest, the restoration of seven miles of stream banks – and so much more. Your contribution makes The Parklands possible.

Membership

Our Members sustain the vision of The Parklands and empower its purpose as a vehicle for attracting and retaining individuals, families, and employers in our area. We rely on Membership donations, not tax dollars, to subsidize our annual operations. Membership contributions are 100% tax-deductible, and they help pay for planting thousands of forbs and shrubs, providing amenities to encourage healthy lifestyles, and engaging tens of thousands of visitors in scientific learning, among other vital benefits.
