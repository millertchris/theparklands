Lowland Plain Trail

Broad Run Park

Paved, fully accessible for walkers, runners, and bicyclists, the Lowland Plain Trail runs from the Greensward down to the Cliffside Paddling Access, where it connects to the Louisville Loop. Double back on the Lowland Plain, or use the Louisville Loop to circle back to your starting point.
