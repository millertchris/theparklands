Scott DeJarnette

Scott DeJarnette joined The Parklands team in August of 2018. In this role, he patrols all four parks during peak visitation and special events to help ensure park guests have a safe and fun experience. Scott retired from Louisville Metro Corrections Department as a Sergeant after 20 years. He is happily married with a daughter and a stepson. He enjoys traveling with his wife and the outdoors—fishing, hiking, and biking—so when he is not working at the park, he is usually in the park doing one of the above.
